Overview

Screen Me Sun Aware Camp Program is an initiative by the Melanoma Network of Canada to educate staff and campers about the importance of sun safety. I worked on the design and development of this online course.

The Canadian Dermatology Association selected the Screen Me Sun Aware Camp Program as their recipient of the 2017 Public Education Award in the Not-for-Profit category.

Project Details

Screen Me is a digital learning + back end registration project which consisted of a 10 minute learning module that gets sent out to camps and counsellors. It teaches them about the importance of sun safety so that they can keep their campers safe from the risks of the sun.
