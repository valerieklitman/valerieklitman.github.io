About Me

Hi, I'm Valerie

I'm a user experience designer and front-end developer. I have a deep interest in our relationship with technology and how it can be used to improve our everyday experiences like learning, shopping and banking. I am a fierce advocate for the user and love working with teams to unravel difficult problems. The more challenging the problem, the more I enjoy solving it!

I have worked on projects for TD Bank, Scotia Bank, Ontario Human Rights Commission, University of Florida, Novartis Pharmaceuticals, Canadian Tire, Ontario Lottery and Gaming Corporation, the Ontario Ministry of Education and more.
